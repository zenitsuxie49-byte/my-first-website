GALIAN 2026

Husay ng CGCIans, Gabay sa Kinabukasan

Day 1: Kickoff and Brainpower

Opening Ceremony Highlights

The school grounds, which we usually just walk through to get to class, totally transformed today! The Opening Program kicked off with so much energy—you could really feel the excitement during the Sibol performance. It made me feel proud to be a CGCIan. To make things even better, the market place opened up right after, and the whole place smelled like our favorite street foods. It was great seeing our fellow students getting creative and running their own little businesses.

Credits to I CORE | Robotics Expo

Later on, things got a bit more serious (but still fun!) at the BSCS Robotics Expo (MOBOT 2026). We were all on the edge of our seats watching the cars try to navigate the maze. Every time a car move a sharp turn without crashing, the whole room would go crazy! Day 1 definitely proved that our tech students aren't just about books—they’ve got the skills to build some pretty amazing things.

Day 2: K-6 Theater Play and Cultural Festival.

Credits to I CORE | K-6 Theater Play

The morning started off super cute with the K-6 Theater Play. It was honestly heartwarming to see the younger kids performing with so much heart. But as afternoon hit, the "sigawan" was back! You could hear the cheers from across the campus as the music started for the next events.

Credits to I CORE | Cultural Festival

As the sun started to set, everyone gathered for the Cultural Festival. The costumes were so colorful, and the dancing was just incredible. Between the performances, we were all just hanging out, sharing food, and taking a million photos at the booths to remember the day. Seeing the stage light up one last time really showed how much talent we have here at CGCI. It was the perfect way to end everything!

"So proud of everyone who participated. Galian 2026 was definitely one for the books!"
